Our Story

Home • Our Story
Since 2009

A Legacy of Hospitality

What began as a simple dream to share the beauty of our home with the world has blossomed into a beloved retreat for travelers from across the globe. Guestland Homestay was founded with a singular vision: to create a space where guests could experience the warmth of genuine hospitality while enjoying the comforts of a luxury retreat.

Our founders, the Anderson family, transformed their ancestral home into what you see today – a harmonious blend of traditional architecture and modern amenities. Every corner of our property tells a story, from the century-old oak tree in our garden to the handcrafted furnishings that adorn each room.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of hosting thousands of guests, each leaving their mark on our journey and inspiring us to continuously improve and innovate.
