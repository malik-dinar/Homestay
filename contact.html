FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability. Please contact us in advance to request.

Do you offer airport pickup service?

Yes, we offer airport pickup and drop-off services at an additional charge. Complimentary pickup is available for stays of 3 nights or more. Please provide your flight details at least 24 hours in advance.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a one-night charge. No-shows will be charged the full booking amount.

Are pets allowed at the homestay?

Yes, we are pet-friendly! Small pets are welcome with prior arrangement. An additional cleaning fee of $25 per night applies. Please inform us at the time of booking.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes! A complimentary full breakfast is included with all room bookings. We offer both local and international options, and can accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, bank transfers, and cash. A valid credit card is required at check-in for incidentals.
